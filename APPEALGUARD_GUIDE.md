## How to Use AppealGuard

### Step 1: Start AppealGuard

In the chat interface, type:
```
/appeal
```
or
```
/appeal start
```

### Step 2: Select Appeal Type

Choose the type of appeal you need:
- Academic Misconduct
- Financial Aid Appeal
- Grade Appeal
- Disciplinary Action

### Step 3: Choose Response Tone

Select the tone that matches your situation:

- **Contrite & Reform-Oriented**: Admitting fault, showing remorse and growth
- **Factual & Evidence-Based**: Denying fault with evidence and logic
- **Urgent & Need-Based**: Emphasizing financial hardship
- **Procedural & Technical**: Focusing on process violations

### Step 4: Select AI Model

Choose the AI model for generation:

- **Claude 4.5 Sonnet** üéØ: Best for nuanced legal writing, empathetic tone

Fill in:
1. **Institution Name**: e.g., "University of Michigan"
2. **Specific Issue**: e.g., "Plagiarism in CS101 Final Project"

## Writing Tips

### DO:

‚úÖ **Be Honest**: The AI removes emotion, so be truthful about what happened  
‚úÖ **Be Specific**: Include dates, course names, assignment titles, amounts  
‚úÖ **Include Context**: Mental health issues, family emergencies, illness  
‚úÖ **Show Growth**: Explain what you've learned and steps taken  
‚úÖ **Mention Support**: Tutoring, counseling, academic advising sought  

### DON'T:

‚ùå **Don't Lie**: False claims will hurt your case  
‚ùå **Don't Blame Others**: Take responsibility or present facts objectively  
‚ùå **Don't Be Vague**: "I had problems" ‚Üí "My mother was hospitalized for 3 weeks"  
‚ùå **Don't Demand**: "I deserve" ‚Üí "I respectfully request consideration"  
‚ùå **Don't Ramble**: Stay focused on the specific issue

## Troubleshooting

### "AppealGuard not loaded"
**Solution:** Refresh the page. The scripts may not have loaded properly.

**Solution:** Generate an appeal first using the wizard.

**Solution:** Make sure you're signed into Puter. Click "Sign In" in the app.

## Best Practices

### Before Submitting

1. **Review Carefully**: Read the entire letter
2. **Personalize**: Add your real name, ID, specific dates
3. **Verify Facts**: Double-check all claims
4. **Attach Evidence**: Include supporting documentation
5. **Proofread**: Fix any typos or errors
6. **Get Feedback**: Show to a trusted advisor or friend

### After Submitting

1. **Follow Up**: Note submission date and method
2. **Keep Copies**: Save confirmation emails
3. **Be Patient**: Appeals take time to review
4. **Stay Engaged**: Check email for committee questions
